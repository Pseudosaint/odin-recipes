Adobo

Home

Description

Adobo is a Filipino meal made with meat, peppercorns, vinegar, and soy sauce. It wad traditionally cooked for the sake of preserving meats for a long time, before refrigeration. This function remains and persists in most recipes, as it remains rich in salt and acid, though it may be modified to the cook's taste.

Ingredients

  • Pork belly, cut into cubes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Peppercorns
  • Vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Fry your aromatics.
  2. Fry the pork.
  3. Add the vinegar, soy sauce, and water to deglaze.. Add until the pork is covered, and to taste.
  4. Add peppercorns.
  5. Add sugar
  6. Wait until the pork is soft.
  7. Add boiled eggs
  8. Let cook until pork is the right tenderness (depends on the cook.)
  9. Let cool.
  10. Serve with any side, preferably rice.
