Description

Sinigang is a Filipino sour soup. It's rich, and traditionally soured with tomatoes and tamarind, though some recipes use calamansi, bilimbi, or other acidic fruits. This recipe uses pork, but fish and other meats are used.

Ingredients

  • Pork belly, cut into cubes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Peppercorns
  • Tamaring powder
  • Water spinach
  • Radish
  • Tomatoes
  • Stringbeans

Instructions

  1. Prepare your meat and vegetables.
  2. Put water in your pot.
  3. Boil your meat.
  4. Then add your aromatics, pepper, salt, and tamarind powder..
  5. Add tomatoes.
  6. Wait until the pork is soft.
  7. Add the vegetables. This is necessary to not break down their structural compounds.
  8. Baste the top of the egg if it is still uncooked.
  9. Let cool.
  10. Serve with any side, preferably rice.
